## ğŸš§ Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: No Market Demand

**Mitigation:**
- Validate with free modules first
- Build email list pre-launch (100+ proves interest)
- Beta test with real users
- Generous refund policy (learn from refunds)

### Risk 2: Competition

**Mitigation:**
- First mover advantage (no comprehensive Restate Go tutorial exists)
- Continuous updates (stay current)
- Community moat (engaged users won't leave)
- High quality bar (can't be easily replicated)

### Risk 3: Restate Adoption Stays Low

**Mitigation:**
- Broaden to distributed systems generally
- Add Temporal, Dapr modules
- Partner with Restate team (get featured)

### Risk 4: Overwhelming Support

**Mitigation:**
- Community-driven support
- Comprehensive FAQ
- Tiered support (Pro gets priority)
- Hire part-time help if needed
