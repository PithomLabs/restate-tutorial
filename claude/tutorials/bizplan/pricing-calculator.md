## ðŸ’° Pricing Models Comparison

### Option 1: One-Time Purchase (RECOMMENDED)

**Pro:**
- Simple for customer (one decision)
- Higher perceived value (lifetime access)
- Easier to sell ($99 vs $9/month forever)
- Lower refund friction
- Scales with time (passive income)

**Con:**
- No recurring revenue
- Customer may not complete (no ongoing commitment)
- Hard to justify ongoing support costs

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Price | What's Included | % of Customers |
|------|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Modules 1-4, Read-only Discord | 70% |
| **Pro** | $99 | All 12 modules, Full Discord, Lifetime updates | 25% |
| **Team** | $499 | 5 licenses, Priority support, Custom requests | 5% |

**Revenue per 100 visitors:**
- 20 email signups (20%)
- 1 Pro purchase (5% of signups) = $99
- 0.1 Team purchase (0.5% of signups) = $50
- **Total: $149 per 100 visitors**

### Option 2: Subscription

**Pro:**
- Recurring revenue (predictable)
- Ongoing relationship
- Easier to justify low monthly price
- Better for SaaS mindset

**Con:**
- Churn (you lose customers every month)
- More complex (billing, cancellations)
- Lower lifetime value if high churn
- Need continuous new content

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Monthly | Annual (2 mo free) | What's Included |
|------|---------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | Modules 1-4 |
| **Pro** | $19/mo | $190/year | All modules, access while subscribed |
| **Pro+** | $29/mo | $290/year | + Priority support |

**Revenue per 100 visitors:**
- 20 email signups
- 2 Pro subscriptions Ã— $19 = $38/month
- **Total: $38 MRR** (but compounds monthly)

**Break-even vs one-time:**
- One-time: $99 immediately
- Subscription: $19/mo Ã— 5 months = $95
- **Conclusion:** Subscription only better if customer stays > 6 months

### Option 3: Hybrid (Best of Both?)

**Model:**
- One-time purchase: $149
- OR Subscription: $15/month
- Let customer choose

**Issue:** Creates decision paralysis, may reduce conversions

### Option 4: GitHub Sponsors

**Model:**
- All content free on GitHub
- Support via GitHub Sponsors ($5, $25, $100/mo tiers)
- Perks at each tier

**Pro:**
- Aligns with open-source values
- Builds goodwill
- Potentially higher volume

**Con:**
- Lower revenue per user
- Relies on generosity (not value exchange)
- Less predictable
